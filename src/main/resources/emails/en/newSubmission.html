Thank you for reporting your giant sea bass encounter! We will use your information to assist scientific research and conservation initiatives for giant sea bass.

The information that you have submitted represents an "encounter" that we track. Each encounter is assigned a unique number, and you can view that encounter at any time using the link above or by going to Spotting Giant Sea Bass. We will keep you informed of any changes to your submitted encounter and email you if the animal is matched to another one within the Spotting Giant Sea Bass database. We will also let you know if/when and where your fish is resighted by other community members.

Researchers at UC Santa Barbara will continue to track whether your giant sea bass is sighted again by other users of Spotting Giant Sea Bass. This information helps us track population trends for this endangered fish and understand more about what habitats are important to this species. Thank you for this contribution and we encourage you to continue to submit more images to help improve this research project.
